About KNR Drone

KNR Drone is a cutting-edge project focused on developing innovative drone solutions for the future. Our team combines expertise in robotics, aerodynamics, and software engineering to create autonomous aerial systems.

From initial concept and CAD modeling to real-world flight testing, we're pushing the boundaries of what's possible in drone technology. Our project represents months of research, simulation, and hands-on development.

We believe in the power of autonomous flight to transform industries, and we're committed to building reliable, efficient, and intelligent drone systems that meet the challenges of tomorrow.

Drone Development

Concept & Requirements

We define the mission, payload, flight time, and regulatory constraints. The goal is a clear spec before any hardware decisions.

Design & Simulation

Airframe layout, component selection, and simulation loops to validate thrust-to-weight, stability, and power budget.

Prototype Build

Rapid mechanical assembly, wiring, and firmware bring-up. We instrument the craft to measure vibration, current draw, and temperatures.

Ground Tests & Tuning

Calibration, PID tuning, failsafe verification, and range checks. Safety gates ensure predictable behavior before first flight.

Maiden Flight & Validation

Flight testing validates performance targets. We iterate based on logs and deliver a reliable platform ready for real missions.
